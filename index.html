A little bit about ME

I have been painting since 2019…

I am a Software Engineer who enjoys painting in my free time. I've always enjoyed sketching since, I was a child. I am inspired by very well-known artists' and hope to become one someday.

Painting has eventually become a medium of expressing vividness & enlighten oneself. Art is a form, whether creating or observing helps in reducing one’s stress hormones and enhances mood

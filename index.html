A little bit about ME

I've Been Making Them Since 2019

I am a software engineer who enjoys painting in my free time. I've always loved sketching since, I was a child. Recently, I found myself becoming more and more creative. I am inspired by very well-known artist and hope to become one someday.

Painting has eventually become a medium of expressing my thoughts as well as ideas. It is like expressing the artistic mind of mine. Like people say art is a form, whether creating or observing which helps in reducing the stress hormone. Indeed painting my ideas enlightens my mood.

There are some of my artworks that I have put together, do take a look around. If you like my artwork do Supoort and Follow me on Instagram. Link is in the contact section. Thank you for your LOVE and SUPPORT!
